APPLE INC. (AAPL) - ADDITIONAL BUSINESS CONTEXT
Data Source: Fiscal Year 2023 Annual Report (10-K)
Period Ended: September 30, 2023

1. GEOGRAPHIC REVENUE BREAKDOWN
While the CSV details product segments, Apple's geographic performance is distinct:
- Americas: Largest market contributor.
- Europe: Second largest market.
- Greater China: Critical for both sales and supply chain.
- Japan & Rest of Asia Pacific: Developing markets.

2. MARGIN ANALYSIS
- Gross Margin generally differs significantly between segments. 
- The 'Services' segment typically commands a much higher gross margin (often ~70%) compared to 'Products' (often ~35-37%).
- As the revenue share of Services grows, Apple's overall company gross margin tends to expand.

3. MARKET SHARE DATA DISCLAIMER
- Apple stopped reporting unit sales figures (the exact number of iPhones, Macs, etc. sold) in 2018.
- The "Market Share" figures in the CSV are estimates based on third-party market research (e.g., IDC, Counterpoint Research, Canalys) and can fluctuate quarterly.

4. EMERGING SEGMENTS
- Apple Vision Pro (Spatial Computing): Launched subsequent to this reporting period, revenues for this product fall under the 'Wearables, Home, and Accessories' category.
